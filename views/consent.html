Consent to Participate in a Research Study

  • Title of the Project: Understanding effects of text messages on decision outcomes
  • Principal Investigator: Alireza Karduni
  • Co-investigator: Mitra Mostafavi
  • Faculty Advisor: Doug Markant

You are invited to participate in a research study. Participation in this research study is voluntary. The information provided is to give you key information to help you decide whether or not to participate.

  • The purpose of this study is to understand how much different text messages effect your decision making on specific topics


  • for four different topics:
    you will first see the topic question, then you will be asked about your knowledge and stance towards that topic
    then you will click through a series of messages, whenever you are ready to make a decision,
    you can do so by clicking decide


  • You must be age 18 or older to participate in this study.
  • You are asked to complete a survey asking a series of short general demographic questions. They are not personal or sensitive.
  • It will take you about 10 minutes to complete the study.
  • We do not believe that you will experience any risk from participating in this study.
  • Your privacy will be protected and confidentiality will be maintained to the extent possible. No Identifiable information will be stored along with your responses and all of your responses are anonymous. We might use the survey data for future research studies and we might share the non-identifiable survey data with other researchers for future research studies without additional consent from you.
  • Besides, Amazon MTurk Compensation, you will not benefit personally by participating in this study. What we learn about how people are motivated may be beneficial to others. Your privacy will be protected and confidentiality will be maintained to the extent possible.
